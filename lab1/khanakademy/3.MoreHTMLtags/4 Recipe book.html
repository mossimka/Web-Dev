Tomato Tofu (with Garlic)

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
firm tofu 400 grams
tomatoes 300 grams
garlic cloves 3
soy sauce 2 tablespoons
olive oil 2 tablespoons
salt to taste
pepper to taste

Step 1: Cut the tofu into cubes and sauté in olive oil until golden brown.

Step 2: Add chopped garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 3: Add diced tomatoes and cook until soft.

Step 4: Stir in soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 5: Serve warm with rice or noodles.

Hachapury

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
flour 500 grams
milk 250 ml
cheese (Suluguni or mozzarella) 300 grams
butter 50 grams
eggs 2
yeast 1 packet
salt to taste

Step 1: Dissolve yeast in warm milk and let it activate for 10 minutes.

Step 2: Mix flour, salt, and yeast mixture to form a dough. Knead for 10 minutes.

Step 3: Let the dough rise for 1 hour in a warm place.

Step 4: Roll out the dough and place grated cheese in the center. Fold the edges over the cheese.

Step 5: Bake at 200°C (390°F) for 20-25 minutes. Add a cracked egg on top 5 minutes before it's done.

Okroshka

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
boiled potatoes 3
boiled eggs 4
cucumbers 2
radishes 100 grams
cooked meat (optional) 200 grams
kefir or kvass 1 liter
fresh herbs (dill, parsley) to taste
salt to taste

Step 1: Dice boiled potatoes, eggs, cucumbers, radishes, and meat (if using).

Step 2: Mix all diced ingredients in a large bowl.

Step 3: Pour kefir or kvass over the mixture and stir well.

Step 4: Add chopped fresh herbs, salt, and stir again.

Step 5: Serve chilled.
